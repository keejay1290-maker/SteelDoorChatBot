About Us

Doing Things The Right Way

Sam Hackett — Manufacturing

Sam comes from a manufacturing family with over four decades in the industry — his father built Leamore Windows from the ground up, starting in 1985. That background instilled an obsession with build quality and doing things properly. At Steel Door Company, Sam brings that same manufacturing-first discipline: every door leaves the factory right, or it doesn't leave at all.

Josh — Digital & Customer Experience

Josh runs Grow.Online, a digital agency. The real spark behind Steel Door Company came from a personal experience: Josh ordered bifold doors and found the whole process frustrating, opaque, and impersonal. He co-founded SDC to prove there was a better way — one where the customer always knows what's happening, what it costs, and why.

How It Works

From Enquiry to Installation

From initial enquiry through to completion, every stage is clearly defined — so you know exactly what to expect throughout.

1
Enquiry
Every project begins with a conversation. We take the time to understand your space and provide a clear estimate, typically within 24 hours.
2
Survey
If you're happy to proceed, we arrange a site survey to take final measurements and confirm all details. We ask that you're present for this stage.
3
Design
We finalise your specification — RAL colour, glass type, hardware finish, and door configuration — before moving to manufacture.
4
Manufacture
Your doors are manufactured to exact specification. Lead times are 6–10 weeks depending on product type and complexity.
5
Installation
Our own installation team fits your doors. We don't subcontract — the people who built it are the people who install it.
6
Aftercare
24-month warranty on all installations. We're here after the job is done — just call the same number you started with.
